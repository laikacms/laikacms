This starter has no framework — just `node:http` + `createEmbeddedLaika`. Read `src/server.ts`
end-to-end (it's ~80 LOC) to understand what every other backend starter is doing under the hood.
Hono/Express/Fastify/Koa all wrap this same pattern with router niceties and middleware.
