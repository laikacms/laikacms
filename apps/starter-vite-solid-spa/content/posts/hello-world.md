This starter is the Solid.js counterpart to the Vue SPA — same architecture (Vite-built client +
sidecar Hono backend, stitched via Vite proxy), but using Solid's signal-based reactivity instead of
Vue's. `createResource` does the fetching, `Show` and `For` do the conditional rendering. No virtual
DOM.
