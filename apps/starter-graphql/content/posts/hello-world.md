This starter wraps LaikaCMS documents as a GraphQL schema. The same underlying repos
(`laika.documents.*`) power both surfaces — JSON:API at `/api/decap/*` and GraphQL at `/graphql`.
ADR-002 sketched this idea; this starter demonstrates it concretely.
