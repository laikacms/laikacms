Subscribe a URL to receive HMAC-signed POSTs every time a post is added, changed, or removed. Useful
for triggering downstream rebuilds (Vercel deploy hook, Cloudflare cache purge), sending
notifications (Slack, Discord), or syncing to external systems (CRM, analytics).
