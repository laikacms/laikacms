This Hono backend starter is **headless** — it exposes the LaikaCMS content over JSON:API and a
Decap admin at `/admin`, but it doesn't render any HTML pages of its own. Point your favorite
frontend (Astro, Nuxt, an iOS app, whatever) at `http://localhost:3000/posts` and render the content
however you like.
